Land Rover Range Rover

Land Rover Range Rover

The Land Rover Range Rover has been chairing as the brand's flag-bearing luxury SUV for more than half a century now. Over the decades, the Range Rover has evolved from a hardcore luxury off-roader into more of an ultra-luxurious flagship SUV. Currently, in its fifth generation, the Land Rover Range Rover is a state-of-the-art machine packed with all the modern tech and luxury features. The 2024 Land Rover Range Rover has arrived with some notable changes, including upgraded powertrain options. The foremost upgrade has been endowed upon the range-topping 2024 Land Rover Range Rover SV trim, which is now powered by a 606-hp V8 motor. SV stands for SVO, which expands to Special Vehicle Operations, a division of Land Rover responsible for creating high-performance, ultra-luxurious and bespoke SUVs. The most capable and opulent Land Rovers are ennobled with the SV designation, showcasing the pinnacle of what the British brand has to offer. The brand-new Land Rover Range Rover SV dwells at the acme of the 2024 Range Rover food chain. It bears a $209,000 starting price and proffers the absolute best of performance, features, interior extravagance, and customization options. Exotic Car Trader has composed this 2024 Land Rover Range Rover SV review, incorporating everything you need to know about the latest and greatest flagship SUV.

Lineage And Legacy Of The Range Rover SV

The very first Range Rover model to be aggrandized by the SV appellation was the 2016 Land Rover Range Rover SVAutobiography. Belonging to the fourth-generation Range Rover lineage, the 2016 Range Rover SVAutobiography featured an abundance of exclusive features. It boasted a standard long-wheelbase and 4-seater rear executive class seating package with additional power adjustments, and massage function. Power came from the top-of-the-line 5.0-liter supercharged V8 motor, which churned out a maximum of 550 horses. The subsequent year, Land Rover added a sportier SV trim to the lineup, dubbed SVAutobiography Dynamic. The 2017 Land Rover Range Rover SVAutobiography Dynamic was a high-performance iteration, featuring lowered ride height and a more aggressive suspension setup. For 2018, both the SV models received a slight bump in power, boosting the output to 557 horsepower. After the introduction of the latest, fifth-generation Range Rover in 2022, the SV badge returned in 2023 without being glued to the Autobiography moniker. The 2023 Land Rover Range Rover SV was positioned at the top of the lineup, with extensive customization options available to choose from. Power was drawn from a turbocharged 4.4-liter V8 motor designated with the powertrain code P530, which produced 523 hp and 553 lb-ft of maximum power and torque. For the 2024 model year, Land Rover has further upgraded the Range Rover SV, with an even more powerful version of this V8 motor.

Motivated By A New 606-HP Twin-Turbo V8

The new 2024 Land Rover Range Rover SV is propelled by an exclusive powertrain codenamed “P615”. This formidable 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 motor is capable of convoking 606 horses and generating 553 lb-ft of maximum torque. This makes the 2024 Range Rover SV officially the most powerful Range Rover flagship ever created. On the brand’s current lineup, it ranks just below the 626-hp 2024 Land Rover Range Rover Sport SV in terms of sheer power output. Power is channeled to all four wheels of the SV via an 8-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel-drivetrain. Land Rover claims that the Range Rover SV dashes to 60 mph in just 4.3 seconds while being limited to a top speed of 162 mph. The SV has a curb weight of 5,985 lbs and, according to the EPA, returns a combined fuel efficiency of 18 MPG.

What's New For 2024?

Apart from the range-topping SV inheriting an all-new powertrain, Land Rover has introduced some other novelties for 2024. The most pivotal one is the upgraded infotainment system, running on the latest iteration of Land Rover's Pivi Pro user interface. Another innovative breakthrough is the new SV bespoke program, which allows customers to extensively customize their Land Rover SUVs. To garner attention to this bespoke program, Land Rover has also revealed a unique and exclusive model named Carmel Edition, limited to just seven units. Priced at an otherworldly $371,475, and based on the long-wheelbase SV trim, the 2024 Land Rover Range Rover SV Carmel Edition showcases a slew of exclusive touches. For starters, the SUV flaunts an “SV Bespoke Special Effect Gloss White” shade and 23-inch wheels with unique white inserts. Inside its prestigious cabin, the Carmel Edition has a four-seater layout, finished in dual-tone Perlino and Deep Garnet leather.
