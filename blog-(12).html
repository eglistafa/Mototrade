2022 Bentley Bentayga Hybrid Review

2022 Bentley Bentayga Hybrid Review

Fancy the Bentley Bentayga's luxury but with a sense of environmental responsibility? The 2022 Bentley Bentayga Hybrid is an excellent choice for drivers who value what the Bentayga model lineup offers while being environmentally conscious and sharing Bentley's going-electric goals, which aim towards the future electrification of the entire Bentley range. Curious how the Bentayga Hybrid compares to other Bentayga models, this 2022 Bentley Bentayga review explores its offerings while hinting at how it compares to its siblings.

Sustainable Luxury

Until the Bentley Bentayga Hybrid was added to the Bentayga lineup in 2018, Bentley had never used a V6 in any of their production cars. The automaker had a long-standing tradition of sticking with their bigger and thirster 6.75-liter engine. The closest they ever came to a V6 was by borrowing Volkswagen's W12 engine, constructed from two narrow V6 engines, which debuted in the Bentley Continental GT in 2003. Back to the Bentayga Hybrid. At its debut, it represented Bentley's first step on a journey towards an electric future, and to the motoring world, it was the first true luxury hybrid. Sure, the Bentayga V8, launched in the same year, captured the hearts of most. But the Bentayga Hybrid wouldn't have remained relevant through its production years if it didn't cater to a niche clientele, the kind of buyers who want to avoid a gas station stop on every other errand.

The Perfect Fusion Of Power And Efficiency

Powering the 2022 Bentley Bentayga Hybrid is a 335-horsepower 3.0-liter V6 that employs a twin-scroll turbocharger, which, in addition to reduced turbo lag, also has fuel efficiency benefits. We won't bore you with the details on that. Together with the 126-horsepower electric motor lodged between the engine and 8-speed transmission, the hybrid powertrain produces a decent 443 horsepower and 516 lb.ft. This is enough to drive the Bentayga Hybrid to 60 mph in 5.5 seconds and to a top speed of 158 mph on all four wheels. The added weight from the battery pack underneath the cabin can't be ignored. It adds around 500 pounds more, scaling the 2022 Bentayga Hybrid's weight to just over 5,800 pounds. But it's a fair exchange, considering you can drive on electric-only mode for around 25 miles.

Embrace The Future In Pure Splendor

Under the hood, the 2022 Bentley Bentayga embraces hybrid technology, while the luxurious interior experience remains unchanged, mirroring the comfort and elegance found in other Bentayga models. You'll only notice the difference in the intelligent hybrid system controls but other than that, everything is as you'd find in the Bentayga V8 or any other Bentayga. All seats are finished in exquisite leather, and the front seats boast the ultimate comfort package with multi-level heating, cooling, and extensive adjustability. The rear seats only have heating functionality, but it's better than nothing since most, in most SUVs, it's a charged addition.
